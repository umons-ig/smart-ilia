Projects

Our ongoing research spans intelligent systems, applied machine learning, and data-driven solutions for real-world problems.

Current Projects
01

WalleSmart

A cloud-based smart farming platform deployed in Wallonia, Belgium. Built on a scalable Lambda architecture combining Apache Kafka Streams and Apache Spark, WalleSmart integrates IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and weather data. Validated across 30 farms and 32 weather stations, it includes decision support tools such as SALVE (veterinary alerting), W@llHerbe (satellite-based pasture monitoring), and DigiMilk (dairy sustainability tracking). Funded by the Walloon Region and led by Elevéo.

Smart Farming IoT Big Data 2018–2026
02

AI4BRAIN

Research project integrating AI tools for statistical analysis, data classification, prediction, and numerical modelling into the care pathway of patients with chronic psychiatric disorders or degenerative neurological diseases, primarily Alzheimer's. Funded by FEDER Wallonie.

Medical AI Neuroimaging Deep Learning 2023–2026
03

ARIAC

Application and Research in Confidence AI, part of the DigitalWallonia4.ai initiative. Focused on developing trustworthy and explainable AI systems for real-world deployment. Funded by the Walloon Region.

Trustworthy AI Explainability 2021–2026
04

TSV — Track Centry Vision

Computer vision project for automated tracking and monitoring systems. Funded by the Walloon Region.

Computer Vision Tracking 2025–2028
05

SiTies — Secure IoT for Smart Cities

International collaboration with Poznan University of Technology (Poland), University of Nis (Serbia), and Universiapolis (Morocco) on securing IoT infrastructure for smart city applications. Funded by NATO Science for Peace and Security programme.

IoT Security Smart Cities NATO
06

ROAD-STEP

Decision Support System Network for herd supervision in grasslands. Strengthens pasture-based livestock farming in Wallonia through remote monitoring tools based on electronic identification of cattle. Funded by the Walloon Region.

Agriculture Decision Support 2018–2024
